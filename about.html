Welcome to Grocefy!

EFFICIENCY:

"Grocefy offers you to get groceries online and have them delivered to your house in no time."

PURPOSE DRIVEN:

"Due to online grocery selling websites like Bigbasket,Grofers and Jio-Mart,
many small grocery stores suffered loss during pandemic.
Grocefy provides an interface for this grocery businessess to sell their products online."

BENEFITS:

"Different grocery stores will be linked to our website,each with its own set of merchandise."
